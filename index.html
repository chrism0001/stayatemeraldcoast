The Home

A Relaxing Coastal Getaway

A peaceful 4-bedroom, 2-bath home in Navarre's quiet Sound Hammock neighborhood — close to beaches, great restaurants, and steps from Santa Rosa Sound.

🏖️

Semi-Private Beach & Pier

Walk to the Sound Hammock neighborhood beach and pier — your own quiet stretch of shoreline on Santa Rosa Sound, through a beautiful boardwalk.

🛶

4 Kayaks Included

Explore the sound with four kayaks and a variety of life vests. Paddle out and experience the Emerald Coast from the water.

🍳

Gourmet Kitchen

Samsung Bespoke appliances, full cookware, coffee maker & grinder, blender, kitchen island, and a gas grill on the screened patio.

🎮

Games & Entertainment

Board games, yard games, 3 smart TVs with DVD players, plus books for all ages. Kids will love the outdoor play set too.

📶

Fast WiFi & Workspace

High-speed internet throughout. The fourth bedroom has a dedicated desk and chair — perfect for remote work during your stay.

🌿

Nature & Wildlife

Conservation land behind the property. Spot shore birds, visit the sea turtle conservation center, and maybe glimpse a black bear passing through.
